⭐ Key Features

  • Concept: Chemical reactions can occur at vastly different rates.
  • Concept: The reactivity of chemicals is a significant factor in how fast chemical reactions proceed.
  • Concept: Chemical reactions may also be reversible and therefore the effect of different variables needs to be established in order to identify how to maximise the yield of desired product.
  • Concept: Understanding energy changes that accompany chemical reactions is important for this process.
  • Concept: In industry, chemists and chemical engineers determine the effect of different variables on reaction rate and yield of product.
